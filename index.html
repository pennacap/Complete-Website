Facilities

These are the school's facilities

Science Labs

There are three science laboratories viz. Physics laboratory, Chemistry laboratory and Biology laboratory. The laboratories are equipped with apparatus, models, chemicals and organic specimens for scientific research and development.
Multipurpose Room

The multipurpose room is used for various purposes such as art, crafts, drama, SUPW and quizzing.
Andrews Hall

The Andrewâ€™s Hall, built to commemorate the 150th year of the school, was inaugurated by then Governor of Mumbai, Dr. P.C. Alexander. The morning assemblies, PT classes and after school co-curricular activities are held in this open-aired hall.
Gamaliel Hall

The Gamaliel Hall is named after our longest serving Principal, the Late Mr. Lazarus Gamaliel. It serves as a multimedia centre with professional lighting and sound. The daily morning assemblies, the Inter-House Competitions, seminars and workshops for staff and parents and get-togethers are held in the Gamaliel Hall.
Mackay Hall

The Mackay Hall, named after our first Principal Mr. Adam Mackay, was formerly the chapel. The Mackay Hall is fully air conditioned and is now equipped with multimedia facilities for the students. The hall is also utilized for meetings, presentations and curricular and co-curricular activities.
Computer Labs

There are three computer laboratories for imparting basic computing skills to the primary and middle school students and basic computer programming to students of standards IX-XII. The subject Computer Studies is optional for students of standards IX-XII and compulsory for students of standards I-VIII.
Activity Room

The K.G. activity room, equipped with puzzles and blocks, gives children an interactive learning environment, which helps them develop hand-eye coordination and fine motor skills.

The activity room for the ISC students has equipment for table tennis, chess and carrom.
Libraries

The Libraries of the school are stocked with 30000 books of different genres. In addition e-books (70000) and audio visual CDs are made available to the students on personal computers.
Remedial Department

This Remedial Department assists children who are identified with specific learning difficulties. With the help of special tools and aids, these students are assisted to cope with their work
